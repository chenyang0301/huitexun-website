Quick Guide

Get started in 3 steps | 🌐 查看中文版
01
🔉
Vol− Key
Decrease volume / AI Q&A voice input
🔊
Vol+ Key
Increase volume / Toggle voice translation
🎤
PTT Key
One-click commentary (single-click) / Group PTT (hold)
🔘
Power Key
Power on/off / Stop commentary
📱
Device QR Code
Scan to add "HtxAi Tour Companion" Mini Program & team up
Scan WeChat QR Code to add
"HtxAi Tour Companion" Mini Program
02
🎙️
One-click Commentary
  • 1 Single-click "PTT Key" → one-click commentary on nearby attractions
  • 2 Single-click "Power Key" to stop commentary
🤖
AI Q&A
  • 1 Hold "Vol− Key" for voice query, release to get voice reply
  • 2 Single-click "Power Key" to stop voice reply
🌐
Voice Translation
  • 1 Hold "Vol+ Key" to enable voice translation mode
  • 2 Hold "Vol+ Key" for 2s again to disable voice translation
👨‍🏫
Guide Mode
  • Open "HtxAi Tour Companion" Mini Program, go to "Group" page, set device to Guide Mode

    Double-click "PTT Key" to start Guide Mode; double-click again to stop
🎧
Simultaneous Translation Commentary
  • Open "HtxAi Tour Companion" Mini Program, go to "Group" page, set device to Simultaneous Translation Commentary Mode

    Hold "Vol+ Key" for 2 seconds to start; hold again for 2 seconds to stop
👥
WeChat Team Up
  • Open "HtxAi Tour Companion" Mini Program, go to "Group" page, scan device QR code to team up
📡
Group PTT
  • 1 Hold "PTT Key" to start PTT; team members can talk
  • 2 Release "PTT Key" to listen to teammates
03
Improve Experience
  • ① In the WeChat Mini Program, scan the QR code on the device to bind it, then tap the device to enter its details page
  • ② Enable the device's "Phone-assisted Positioning" switch to improve positioning accuracy
  • ③ Tap "Guide Voice" to select your preferred narration voice
⚠️ Note: The bound device and phone must be carried by the same person, and do not exit WeChat during use
